Primary colors

The Dana-Farber primary colors serve as a foundational palette that work universally across all of the Jimmy Fund’s brand experience.

Consisting of white and monochromatic increments of gray and Dana-Farber Blue and Amber, these colors will anchor and balance color expressions and can be complimented with the accent color palette.
